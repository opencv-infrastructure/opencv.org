Today OpenCV team did a tutorial on "Mobile Computer Vision with OpenCV" at the ICVS 2013 conference. This is the 9th International Conference on Computer Vision Systems, that is taking place in St. Petersburg, Russia during July 16-18, 2013.

We have covered not only OpenCV for iOS and OpenCV for Android/Tegra ports, but also shared some information on OpenCV 3.0 plans. So, if you're interested to know what to expect in the next major OpenCV release, feel free to study attached materials:

  • OpenCV for Android/Tegra
